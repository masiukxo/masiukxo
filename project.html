Projects

Emotions

In February 2017 I took voluntary part in the student project in which I was responsible for make-up of the model. The theme of the photoshop was emotions. My goal was to make a very delicate make up in order to meet the requriements of the photographer and at the same time make it a bit artictic. This can be seen by fake colourfull freckles. The photos were taken by Justyna Solnica, a current student of the Academy of Advertisment in Warsaw.

Festival

In March 2016 I was responsible for the decoration of the school festival. The initiative of the project was voluntary. I designed and managed a wallpaper decoration which presented a comic. Our goal was to create matching decoration for the festival theme as well as fit them into the school's background. The theme of the festival was "Retro style".
